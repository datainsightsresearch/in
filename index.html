About Us

Insights Opinion is a global market research company with over a decade of experience in delivering data collection and analysis services. Our mission is to provide precise, reliable, and actionable insights that help clients make confident business decisions. Our team of experts, advanced technology, and adherence to global standards distinguish us in the industry.

Services

Quantitative Research
  • Global Panel - Access diverse and vetted respondents from around the world.
  • CATI - Computer-assisted telephone interviews for reliable quantitative data.
  • CAPI - Face-to-face interviewing with digital support to ensure accuracy.
  • CLT - Central location testing for products, services, and marketing materials.

Qualitative Research
  • Focus Group Discussions - Understand consumer perceptions through guided group interactions.
  • In-Depth Reviews - Comprehensive one-on-one interviews for detailed insights.
  • In-Home Usage Testings - Evaluate product usability and customer experience in real environments.
  • Mystery Shopping - Assess service quality and compliance through anonymous visits.

Support Services
  • Survey Programming - Efficient design and deployment of surveys using advanced platforms.
  • Translation - Multilingual support to localize research tools and outputs.
  • Data Insights - Statistical analysis and visualization for impactful decision-making.

Research

We employ a mix of traditional and digital research methods to ensure comprehensive data collection and analysis. Our methodologies include online surveys, telephonic interviews, field studies, observational research, and more. We continuously adapt to emerging technologies and trends to maintain accuracy and relevance.

Quality Standard

Our commitment to quality is uncompromising. We follow ISO-certified processes and industry best practices at every stage of the research lifecycle. From survey design to data processing, we implement rigorous quality checks, respondent validation, and result verification to deliver trustworthy outcomes.
